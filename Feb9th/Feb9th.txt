2) AI Around Me

1. Google Search

	Google uses ML to understand user queries. Shows the most relevant websites based on past data and search 	behavior.

2. YouTube Recommendations

	ML analyzes your watch history. Searches patterns to suggest videos you may be interested in.

3. Instagram Feed

	AI studies your interactions (likes, shares, comments).Shows posts similar to your interests.

4. Google Maps

	ML predicts traffic conditions & suggests the fastest route. Uses real-time & historical data.

5. Voice Assistants

	AI uses speech recognition models. Converts voice input into understandable commands.

6. Netflix Recommendations

	ML recommends movies based on your viewing history & similarity with other users.

7. Amazon Shopping Suggestions

	AI analyzes your browsing & purchase history. Recommends products you might buy.

8. Spam Email Filter

	ML identifies spam emails by analyzing patterns from previously marked spam messages.

9. Face Unlock in Mobile

	AI uses image recognition models to identify & match your facial features.

10. Autocorrect / Predictive Text

	ML predicts the next word by learning from large language datasets & your typing history.
