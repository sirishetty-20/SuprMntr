**2) AI Around Me**



**1. Google Search**



&#x09;Google uses ML to understand user queries. Shows the most relevant websites based on past data and search 	behavior.



**2. YouTube Recommendations**



&#x09;ML analyzes your watch history. Searches patterns to suggest videos you may be interested in.



**3. Instagram Feed**



&#x09;AI studies your interactions (likes, shares, comments).Shows posts similar to your interests.



**4. Google Maps**



&#x09;ML predicts traffic conditions \& suggests the fastest route. Uses real-time \& historical data.



**5. Voice Assistants**



&#x09;AI uses speech recognition models. Converts voice input into understandable commands.



**6. Netflix Recommendations**



&#x09;ML recommends movies based on your viewing history \& similarity with other users.



**7. Amazon Shopping Suggestions**



&#x09;AI analyzes your browsing \& purchase history. Recommends products you might buy.



8\. **Spam Email Filter**



&#x09;ML identifies spam emails by analyzing patterns from previously marked spam messages.



**9. Face Unlock in Mobile**



&#x09;AI uses image recognition models to identify \& match your facial features.



**10. Autocorrect / Predictive Text**



&#x09;ML predicts the next word by learning from large language datasets \& your typing history.
